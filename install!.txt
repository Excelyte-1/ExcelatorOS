Installing ExcelatorOS
======================

These instructions will guide you through getting ExcelatorOS onto a USB flash drive or DVD and booting into the live environment. After that, installing the OS is simple — the menus are easy to navigate, just like Windows.

──────────────────────────────────────────
Step 1: Download Balena Etcher
──────────────────────────────────────────
- Download Balena Etcher here: https://etcher.balena.io/  
- Balena Etcher is also sometimes called just "Etcher".

──────────────────────────────────────────
Step 2: Flash the ISO to your USB
──────────────────────────────────────────
1. Open Balena Etcher.  
2. Click **"Flash from file"** and select your ExcelatorOS ISO file.  
3. Click **"Select target"** and choose your USB drive.  
4. Press **"Flash"** and wait for the process to complete.  

Once done, safely eject your USB drive.

──────────────────────────────────────────
Step 3: Boot into the Live Environment
──────────────────────────────────────────
1. Shut down your PC and power it back on.  
2. Immediately start pressing the **Enter key repeatedly** until you see a boot menu.  
3. Select the option that says **"Select temporary boot device"** (or something similar).  
4. Choose your USB drive from the list.  

Your computer will now boot into the ExcelatorOS live environment.

──────────────────────────────────────────
Step 4: Install ExcelatorOS
──────────────────────────────────────────
- Follow the on-screen instructions — the installer is intuitive and simple.  
- Once the installation is complete, reboot your PC.  
- Congratulations! You are now ready to start using ExcelatorOS.
